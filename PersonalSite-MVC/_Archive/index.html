Full-stack developers are capable of developing complete web applications on their own, from start to finish, or working in teams with other professionals to deliver comprehensive solutions. Full-stack developers may also have experience with web frameworks, APIs, and cloud computing platforms to build scalable and robust applications that meet user needs.
